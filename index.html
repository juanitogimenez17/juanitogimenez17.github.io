About JAM VR Travel

Our name “JAM” comes from the names of our team: Jon, Aitor, and Mario. With JAM VR Travel, you will experience a virtual reality visit to three famous places around the world from the comfort of your home.

Locations

1. Warner Bros Madrid

Visit several attractions in this theme park in Madrid, including:

  • Batman Gotham City Escape
  • Superman
  • Roadrunner "Bip Bip"

2. Twin Towers

Experience the Twin Towers from both the outside and inside, feeling like you're back in the 2000s. For an additional 15€, you can even fly around the towers and visit Manhattan as it was in the year 2000.

3. Norway

Witness the natural wonder of the Aurora Polaris, explore the local town, and enjoy breathtaking mountain views.

Why Travel in VR?

Traveling in VR is easier, better for the planet, and more accessible for everyone, including busy individuals and those with disabilities. It's also cheaper than traditional travel and doesn’t contribute to pollution.
